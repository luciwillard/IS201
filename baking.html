Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar (packed)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Stir flour with baking soda and salt; set aside.
  3. In large mixing bowl, beat butter with sugar, and brown sugar at medium speed until creamy and lightened in color.
    • Make sure butter is softened and the brown sugar is packed
  4. Add eggs and vanilla, one at a time. Mix on low speed until incorporated.
  5. Gradually blend dry mixture into creamed mixture. Stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by tablespoon onto ungreased cookie sheets.
    • You can also use a cookie scoop, and that works great!
  7. Bake for 9-11 minutes or until the cookies are golden brown.
